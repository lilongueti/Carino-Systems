What is GNU/Linux速?

It is how you refer to an operating system with the Linux Kernel and they're usually known as "distros", key difference with Windows and macOS is you can see what is it made of, therefore other people can make versions of it that already have software you need. All of your programs are called packages, and they update with the system.

Why GNU/Linux速?

Because you have the choice. You don't have to be dealing with forced updates and system defaults. And you don't need a top of the line computer to do your work, there are few some exceptions though.
